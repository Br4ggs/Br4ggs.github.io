About Me

My name is Emiel van den Brink; a motivated developer who's currently studying for a bachelor in software engineering.

My main interest lies in back-end programming. I enjoy applying algorithms, equations and math to my projects, like terrain creation or dungeon generation. However, I'm always up for an opportunity to experiment and learn something new. I'm not one to turn down a challenge!

There are still many things I want to learn, but I am determined and motivated to do so!
